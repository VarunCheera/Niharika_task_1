About

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on creating systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding language, and recognizing patterns. AI technologies such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision are used in applications like virtual assistants, self-driving cars, and recommendation systems. AI can analyze large amounts of data quickly, making it valuable in fields like healthcare, finance, and education. It enables machines to adapt and improve over time without being explicitly programmed for every task. While AI brings many benefits, it also raises concerns about privacy, bias, and the future of jobs. Despite the challenges, AI is continuously evolving and playing a major role in shaping the future of technology and society.
